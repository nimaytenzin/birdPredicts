Bird Species Prediction App

This Deep Learning Model is trained using the dataset of 260 birds found in USA and yields an accuracy of 96%. The list of birds used for training can be found here.
The predictions for local Birds of Bhutan may not be accurate since the model is trained on US Datasets.

Note: For usage in Bhutan, the model needs to be retrained using the Datasets of birds in Bhutan.

Usage: Clik on the upload Image button.Navigate to the bird image and click on upload. The prediction results will be displayed shortly. Note: For best results, please upload a full body picture of the bird
